STRATEGY CHAT SEED

Executive Summary
The current strategy book has one strongest confirmed daily winner, one strongest operationally mature intraday system, and one strongest paper-only supporting signal family. Down Streak Exhaustion is the most credible all-around strategy on decision-grade evidence: test ending equity 125394.19, CAGR 20.50%, profit factor 1.6426, Sharpe 1.4177, max drawdown 6.05%, and benchmark wins versus SPY, equal_weight_full, and equal_weight_etf, but it is still marked fragile because subset and regime robustness are uneven. QQQ-Led TQQQ/SQQQ Pair Opening-Range Intraday System is the closest to real deployment: adverse validate/test net PnL 922.21 / 1441.14, combined expectancy 16.06, max drawdown about 9.23%, explicit paper runbooks, and quote-aware validation, but promotion is still blocked by concentration and slippage realism. Durable cRSI Family is the best paper-only support layer: 768 stress-test runs, 22 survivors at slippage >= 10 bps, and durable presets that still work at 10 to 15 bps, but every preset is still needs_cleaner_ops because the paper sample is short and overnight/stale-data risk remains. The main upside reserve is in Momentum / Relative-Strength Family and Breakout / Trend-Continuation Family, but both remain promising rather than settled because their strongest rows come from raw search tables rather than final confirmation packets.
